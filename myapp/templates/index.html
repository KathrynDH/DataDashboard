World Bank Data Dashboard

Youth Literacy

Literacy is crucial for individuals and countries as a whole.
This data dashboard displays graphs and charts related to youth (ages 15-24) literacy rates in different countries.
Data Source: World Bank Open Data

The first two graphs display information on youth literacy rates aggregated by income group. You can learn more about the country income groups here:
WDI - The World by Income and Region

For reference, here is the population for each group.

The next two scatter plots relate youth literacy rate and income.
Income is measured as GNI converted to current U.S. dollars. You can read more about GNI here: What is the World Bank Atlas method?
